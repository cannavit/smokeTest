Benefits of using ZipiTest..

Improve your CICD

ZipiTest helps you to make your continuous integration cycle more robust, detecting Bugs inside your containers.

Maintenance free..

ZipiTest does not need maintenance, create automatic tests using the addresses of your APis. This helps reduce time on development teams.

Quick test...

ZipiTest is based on smokeTests. Quick and consistent tests verifying the stability of your environments.

Machine Learning

ZipiTest offers you the possibility to train Machine Learning algorithms to create smarter test scenarios and learn from your developers.

Fast deployments

ZipiTest helps you speed up your deployments in production. By early detection of Bugs.
